Event Details

Get ready for the most magical and spooktacular event of the year!

Date: October 31st - December 25th

Location: Spookyville Park

Admission: Free

Santascares is a unique holiday celebration that brings together the enchantment of Christmas and the thrills of Halloween.

It's a family-friendly event where you can experience the best of both worlds.

Food and Treats

Indulge in delicious holiday treats and eats that will tantalize your taste buds:

  • Creepy Christmas cookies: Spooky-shaped cookies decorated with holiday icing.
  • Witch's brew hot chocolate: Warm up with a cup of our magical hot chocolate.
  • Pumpkin spice candy canes: A fusion of two holiday flavors in one sweet treat.
  • Ghostly gingerbread men: Gingerbread cookies in the shape of Halloween ghosts.
  • Candy apple ornaments: Edible candy apples shaped like Christmas ornaments.
  • Frankenstein's fruitcake: A monster-sized slice of delicious fruitcake.

Don't forget to visit our food stalls and try these delightful concoctions!

Entertainment

We have a lineup of fantastic entertainment for all ages:

  • Spooky storytelling by the fireside
  • Holiday-themed magic shows
  • Live reindeer petting zoo
  • Interactive haunted house experience
  • Christmas light display synchronized to Halloween music
  • Fireworks show every Saturday night

Our entertainment will keep you enchanted throughout the entire event!
